Discord Server Rules

1) Be respectful. Be welcoming to new members!

2) Please don't spam in any of the channels. That means don't take several lines to not say much, and don't say the same thing over and over

3) No pornography (keep it SFW), insults, racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia and other kinds of discriminatory speech.

4) Do not advertise your own stream or YouTube-channel. Do not jointhe server to promote your content.

5) Buying, selling, or trading of accounts or cheats is forlidden.

6) When in discussion, be civin. Sometimes arguments can get heated, but you'll never be able to understand each other by not listening too.

7) No personal information, no addresses, no phone numbers, SSN or NI numbers or anything of those sorts.

8) Do not impersonate league personalities,Rioters, or mods.

9) When in doubt, Moderators and Discord Mods hold final and complete judgement.

Note: Be mindful of reddit's and r/leagueoflegends'rules against vote manipulation. Do not use Discord to promote your reddit post/comment or try to influence voting on posts/comments in any way.
